## Introduction

The Weekly Menu Creation feature allows you to create professional, visually appealing weekly menus for your tiffin service in minutes. Using AI-powered design templates from Canva, you can generate beautiful menus that you can share with customers via WhatsApp, social media, or print.

### What You Can Do

- Upload and manage recipe photos
- Build 6-day weekly menus (Monday-Saturday)
- Generate professional menu designs with AI
- Export menus in multiple formats (PNG, PDF, JPG)
- Share menus with customers via shareable links
- Track which menus are published

### Time Savings

- **Traditional method:** 2-3 hours to design a menu manually
- **With this feature:** 10-15 minutes from start to finish

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

1. **Recipes created** in the Recipe Creation page
2. **Recipe photos** ready to upload (JPG, PNG, or WEBP format)
3. **Canva integration** enabled (automatic with the application)

### Navigation

Access the Weekly Menu feature from the sidebar under the "WEEKLY MENU" section:

- **Recipe Images** - Upload and manage photos
- **Menu Builder** - Create weekly menus
- **Templates** - Generate AI designs

## Step 1: Upload Recipe Images

Recipe images make your menus more appealing and help customers visualize the food.

### How to Upload Images

1. **Navigate** to "Recipe Images" in the sidebar
2. **Select a recipe** from the list on the left
3. **Click "Upload Image"** button
4. **Choose a photo** from your device
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
   - Maximum size: 5MB
   - Recommended: High-quality food photos with good lighting
5. **Wait for upload** - The image will be uploaded to your server and automatically synced to Canva

### Managing Images

#### View Images
- All uploaded images appear in a gallery grid
- Each image shows its file size and Canva status
- Images are organized by recipe

#### Set Default Image
- Click the **star icon** on any image to set it as default
- The default image will be used in menu templates
- Only one image can be default per recipe

#### Delete Images
- Click the **delete icon** (trash can) to remove an image
- Confirm the deletion when prompted
- Deleted images are removed from both server and Canva

### Image Best Practices

**Good Photos:**
- Well-lit with natural lighting
- Close-up shots showing food details
- Clean background (white or neutral)
- High resolution (at least 1000px wide)
- Appetizing presentation

**Avoid:**
- Blurry or out-of-focus photos
- Dark or poorly lit images
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Low resolution images
- Photos with watermarks

## Step 2: Build Weekly Menu

Create a 6-day menu (Monday-Saturday) with multiple items per day.

### Creating a New Menu

1. **Navigate** to "Menu Builder" in the sidebar
2. **Fill in menu details:**
   - **Week Start Date** - The Monday of the menu week (auto-set)
   - **Menu Name** - Optional (e.g., "Diwali Special Week")
   - **Description** - Optional notes about the menu
3. **Add items for each day** (Monday through Saturday)

### Adding Items to a Day

1. **Click "Add Item"** under any category for any day
2. **Select a recipe** from the dialog
3. **The item appears** in that category
4. **Repeat** for all days and categories

### Categories

Each day has 5 categories:

1. **Sabzi** - Vegetable dishes
2. **Dal/Kadhi** - Lentils or yogurt-based dishes
3. **Rice** - Rice preparations
4. **Roti/Paratha** - Bread items
5. **Special Items** - Desserts, snacks, or special dishes

### Managing Menu Items

#### Remove Items
- Click the **X icon** next to any item to remove it
- No confirmation needed

#### Item Count
- Each day card shows the total number of items
- Helps you balance the menu across days

### Saving the Menu

1. **Click "Save Menu"** button at the top
2. **Wait for confirmation** - You'll see a success message
3. **Menu is saved** and ready for template generation

### Menu Best Practices

**Balanced Menu:**
- Include at least 3-4 items per day
- Vary the dishes across the week
- Consider customer preferences
- Balance simple and special items

**Variety:**
- Don't repeat the same dish multiple days
- Mix different cuisines or styles
- Include seasonal vegetables
- Offer vegetarian and vegan options

## Step 3: Generate Templates

Use AI to generate professional menu designs in seconds.

### Generating New Templates

1. **Navigate** to "Templates" in the sidebar
2. **Click "Generate with AI"** button
3. **Choose a style** or enter a custom prompt

### Pre-Defined Styles

**1. Modern**
- Clean layout with neutral colors
- Minimalist design
- Professional look
- Best for: Corporate clients, upscale services

**2. Traditional**
- Decorative borders with warm colors
- Cultural motifs
- Festive appearance
- Best for: Festival weeks, traditional menus

**3. Colorful**
- Vibrant colors with playful fonts
- Eye-catching design
- Fun and energetic
- Best for: Family services, casual dining

**4. Minimalist**
- Simple layout with lots of white space
- Clean typography
- Easy to read
- Best for: Health-conscious customers, simple menus

### Custom Prompts

For unique designs, enter your own description:

**Examples:**
- "Elegant menu with gold accents and floral borders"
- "Rustic menu with wooden texture and handwritten fonts"
- "Modern menu with food photography and gradient background"
- "Traditional Indian menu with mandala patterns and warm colors"

### Selecting a Template

1. **AI generates 4 candidates** for each request
2. **Preview the thumbnails** to see the designs
3. **Click "Save This Template"** on your favorite
4. **Template is saved** to your library

### Managing Templates

#### Set Default Template
- Click the **star icon** to set a template as default
- Default template is used for all new menu exports
- You can change the default anytime

#### Preview in Canva
- Click the **preview icon** (eye) to open in Canva
- Edit the template directly in Canva if needed
- Changes are saved automatically

#### Delete Templates
- Click the **delete icon** to remove a template
- System templates cannot be deleted
- Your custom templates can be deleted anytime

## Step 4: Export and Share

Finalize your menu and share it with customers.

### Exporting Menus

1. **Navigate** to "Export & Share" in the sidebar
2. **Select a menu** from the list on the left
3. **Choose export options:**
   - **Format:** PNG (image), PDF (document), or JPG (image)
   - **Quality:** Low (fast), Medium (balanced), or High (best quality)
4. **Click "Export Menu"**
5. **Wait for export** - The file will open automatically

### Export Format Guide

**PNG (Recommended)**
- Best quality for images
- Transparent background support
- Perfect for WhatsApp and social media
- File size: Medium

**PDF**
- Best for printing
- Professional document format
- Easy to email
- File size: Small

**JPG**
- Universal compatibility
- Smaller file size
- Good for quick sharing
- File size: Small

### Quality Settings

**High (Recommended)**
- Best image quality
- Larger file size
- Use for: Printing, professional sharing
- Export time: Slower

**Medium**
- Balanced quality and size
- Good for most uses
- Use for: WhatsApp, social media
- Export time: Moderate

**Low**
- Fastest export
- Smaller file size
- Use for: Quick previews, testing
- Export time: Fast

### Sharing Menus

1. **Click "Create Share Link"** button
2. **Copy the generated URL** using the copy icon
3. **Share the link** via:
   - WhatsApp
   - Email
   - SMS
   - Social media
   - Website

### Share Link Features

- **Unique URL** for each menu
- **No login required** for customers
- **Mobile-friendly** viewing
- **Always up-to-date** if you update the menu

Generate QR codes for:
- Print menus

## Tips and Best Practices

### Photography Tips

**Lighting:**
- Use natural daylight when possible
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Use diffused light for even illumination

**Composition:**
- Fill the frame with the dish
- Use the rule of thirds
- Include garnishes for color

**Editing:**
- Adjust brightness and contrast
- Enhance colors slightly
- Crop to remove distractions

### Menu Planning Tips

**Weekly Variety:**
- Plan menus 2-3 weeks in advance
- Rotate popular dishes
- Introduce new items gradually

**Customer Preferences:**
- Track which dishes get the most orders
- Ask for feedback regularly
- Adjust menus based on demand

**Seasonal Planning:**
- Use seasonal vegetables
- Plan festival specials
- Adjust for weather (lighter in summer, heartier in winter)

### Design Tips

**Template Selection:**
- Match style to your brand
- Use consistent templates for recognition
- Change styles for special occasions

**Readability:**
- Ensure text is large enough
- Use high contrast colors
- Avoid cluttered designs

**Branding:**
- Add your logo (in Canva)
- Use your brand colors
- Include contact information

### Sharing Tips

**Timing:**
- Share menus 2-3 days before the week starts
- Send reminders on Sunday evening
- Post updates if items change

**Channels:**
- WhatsApp groups for regular customers
- Social media for new customer acquisition
- Email for corporate clients
- Website for general audience

**Engagement:**
- Ask customers to vote on dishes
- Share behind-the-scenes photos
- Highlight special items

## Troubleshooting

### Image Upload Issues

**Problem:** Image upload fails  
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Verify file format (JPG, PNG, or WEBP only)
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try a different browser

**Problem:** Image doesn't appear in gallery  
**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check if upload completed successfully
- Verify the recipe is selected
- Clear browser cache

**Problem:** Canva upload fails  
**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Verify Canva integration is enabled
- Contact support if persists

### Menu Builder Issues

**Problem:** Can't add items to menu  
**Solutions:**
- Ensure recipes are created first
- Check if menu is saved
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser

**Problem:** Items disappear after saving  
**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection during save
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Look for error messages
- Try saving again

**Problem:** Can't delete items  
**Solutions:**
- Click the X icon directly
- Refresh the page if unresponsive
- Save menu after making changes

### Template Generation Issues

**Problem:** AI generation fails  
**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Try a different style
- Simplify custom prompt
- Wait a few minutes and retry

**Problem:** Templates don't appear  
**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check if generation completed
- Look for error messages
- Try generating again

**Problem:** Can't save template  
**Solutions:**
- Ensure you clicked "Save This Template"
- Check internet connection
- Wait for conversion to complete
- Try a different candidate

### Export Issues

**Problem:** Export fails  
**Solutions:**
- Verify menu has a Canva design
- Check internet connection
- Try a different format
- Lower the quality setting

**Problem:** Export file doesn't open  
**Solutions:**
- Check if download completed
- Verify file format is supported
- Try a different browser
- Re-export the menu

**Problem:** Share link doesn't work  
**Solutions:**
- Verify link was copied correctly
- Check if menu is published
- Try generating a new link
- Test link in incognito mode

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How many menus can I create?**  
A: Unlimited. Create as many menus as you need.

**Q: Can I edit a menu after saving?**  
A: Yes, you can update menu details and items anytime.

**Q: Do I need a Canva account?**  
A: No, Canva integration is built-in and automatic.

**Q: Can I use my own templates?**  
A: Yes, you can create custom templates in Canva and import them.

### Image Questions

**Q: What's the best image size?**  
A: At least 1000px wide, 1500px recommended for best quality.

**Q: Can I upload multiple images per recipe?**  
A: Yes, upload as many as you want and set one as default.

**Q: What if I don't have recipe photos?**  
A: You can still create menus without images, but they're recommended.

**Q: Can I edit images in the app?**  
A: Not currently, but you can edit in Canva after upload.

### Menu Questions

**Q: Why only Monday-Saturday?**  
A: Most tiffin services don't operate on Sundays. You can customize this.

**Q: Can I add more than 5 categories?**  
A: Currently limited to 5 categories, but you can request more.

**Q: Can I copy a menu to another week?**  
A: Not yet, but this feature is coming soon.

**Q: How do I delete a menu?**  
A: Use the delete option in the menu list (coming soon).

### Template Questions

**Q: How many templates can I save?**  
A: Unlimited templates in your library.

**Q: Can I edit templates?**  
A: Yes, click "Preview in Canva" to edit directly.

**Q: Do templates include my menu items?**  
A: Templates are design layouts. Menu items are added during export.

**Q: Can I share templates with others?**  
A: Not currently, but you can export and share the design.

### Export Questions

**Q: What's the best format for WhatsApp?**  
A: PNG with high quality works best.

**Q: Can I print the exported menu?**  
A: Yes, use PDF format with high quality.

**Q: How long do share links last?**  
A: Share links are permanent until you delete them.

**Q: Can I track who views my shared menus?**  
A: Not currently, but analytics are coming soon.
